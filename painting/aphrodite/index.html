We present an exceptional artwork that has opened the doors of the prestigious Faculty of Fine Arts at the Complutense University of Madrid for its talented creator. This piece, masterfully executed in charcoal and sanguine on Ingres paper, skillfully fuses elements of two iconic figures from mythology and classical art: the Venus de Milo and Aphrodite.

The majestic statue, rising to 1.60 meters upon its pedestal, features a slender and elegant figure, wrapped in a Greek mantle that reveals a breast in a nod to the classical style. The Greek profile nose and intricate, angelic hairstyle, comprised of curled locks wound into a bun, reflect the mastery and artistic knowledge of its creator.

The penetrating and delicate gaze of the goddess is directed towards a point in space, as if something had captured her divine attention. This detail brings life and depth to the work, connecting the viewer with the artist's mind and heart. The piece, measuring 100 cm high and 75 cm wide, is a striking representation of the power and beauty of classical art, as well as a tribute to the skill and passion of its author.

The delicacy in the execution of the drawing and that transcendent gaze reflect the spirituality of the artist, who created this work during the week of the entrance exam to Fine Arts. Her talent and dedication are evident in each stroke, revealing a soul passionate about art and its profound meaning.

我们现在要介绍的是一件非凡的作品，这件作品为其才华横溢的创作者打开了马德里康普顿斯大学著名的美术学院的大门。这件作品巧妙地融合了两个古典神话和艺术中的标志性人物：米洛的维纳斯和阿佛洛狄忒，它是用木炭和红铅笔在英格雷斯纸上精心制作的。

这座宏伟的雕像高1.60米，矗立在她的基座上，呈现出一个苗条而优雅的形象，身披希腊长袍，赤裸的胸部似乎在向古典风格致敬。希腊侧脸的鼻子和复杂而天使般的发型，由卷曲的卷发组成一个发髻，反映了创作者的艺术技巧和知识。

这位女神深情而细腻的眼神似乎被空间中的某个点所吸引，就好像某个东西已经吸引了她的神圣注意力。这个细节为作品增加了生命力和深度，使观众与艺术家的思想和心灵相连。这件作品高100厘米，宽75厘米，是古典艺术的力量和美感的令人震惊的表现，也是对其作者技巧和激情的致敬。

绘图的细腻和那种超越的眼神反映了艺术家的精神境界，她在美术入学考试的那一周创作了这件作品。她的才华和奉献在每一笔画中都显而易见，揭示了一颗对艺术及其深远意义充满热情的灵魂。
