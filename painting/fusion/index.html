We present an extraordinary work, a divine fusion of religions that will forever change our perception of spirituality.

Allow me to take you on a journey back in time to the year 2000, when Paz Arés Osset, a devout Christian, was immersed in her studies to become a Buddhist teacher. In that same year, she founded the NGO CALCOOP, an organization dedicated to cooperation with Calcutta, India. Her deep love and understanding of both Hinduism and India, as well as Arab countries and Islam, sparked within her a burning love for spiritual paths and the wisdom they embody.

In her heart, these paths intertwined and merged into the unity of her essence, which is love itself. This mystical vision revealed to her a universal truth: all religions, if we transcend the veil of appearances, join hands and, deep down, are the same expression of divine love.

Inspired by this revelation, Paz Arés Osset created a work of art in engraving that captures the essence of her transcendental vision. In this work, we can contemplate the presence of a Buddhist monk and a Catholic nun, their hands intertwined in a gesture of spiritual communion. The Islamic lattice, with its intricate arabesques, illuminates the room, revealing the presence of an angel and Jesus Christ in the trunk of a sacred tree.

Every element of this work conveys a deep connection between different spiritual traditions. The monk holds the bible, symbolizing Christian wisdom and teachings, while the nun reads the Buddhist tablets, representing the pursuit of truth and inner peace. The arabesques in the lattice reveal an angel and Jesus Christ in the tree, thus uniting Christianity with the Islamic tradition.

This work of engraving art is an invitation to reflect on the unity and beauty that resides in spiritual diversity. Paz Arés Osset reminds us that we are one, that all human beings share the same pursuit of peace and happiness. Her prayer calls for world peace, the unity of all beings from different spiritual paths, so that together we may reach bliss and free ourselves from suffering, embraced by love in its unity.

Fall in love with this wonderful creation that challenges the boundaries of art and spirituality. Let it touch your soul and open your eyes to the unity that lies beyond superficial appearances. This transcendental work by Paz Arés Osset is a legacy that will endure through the ages, inspiring future generations to embrace communion and love in all its forms.

You cannot miss the opportunity to witness this masterpiece that merges religions and ignites the flame of unity and peace in our hearts!

我们展示了一部非凡的作品，这是一种宗教的神圣融合，将永远改变我们对精神性的认知。

让我带您回到2000年的时光之旅，当时的Paz Arés Osset是一位虔诚的基督徒，她沉浸在她的研究中，以成为一位佛教教师。同年，她创立了非政府组织CALCOOP，这是一个致力于与印度加尔各答合作的组织。她对印度教和印度以及阿拉伯国家和伊斯兰教的深深的爱和理解，在她心中激起了对精神道路和他们所包含的智慧的热爱。

在她的心中，这些道路交织并融合成她的本质的统一，那就是爱本身。这种神秘的视野向她揭示了一个普遍的真理：所有的宗教，如果我们超越外表的面纱，都会携手并进，在深处，都是神圣爱的同一表达。

受此启发，Paz Arés Osset创作了一部艺术作品，以雕刻的方式捕捉她超然视野的本质。在这部作品中，我们可以看到一个佛教僧侣和一个天主教修女的存在，他们的手以精神交流的手势交织在一起。伊斯兰格子窗，其错综复杂的阿拉伯式花饰照亮了房间，揭示了一位天使和耶稣基督在一棵神圣树干中的存在。

这个作品的每个元素都传达了不同精神传统之间的深深联系。和尚握着圣经，象征着基督教的智慧和教义，而修女阅读佛教经文，代表对真理和内心平静的追求。在格子窗的阿拉伯式花饰中揭示了一个天使和耶稣基督在树中，从而将基督教与伊斯兰传统结合在一起。

这部雕刻艺术作品是对反思精神多样性中的统一和美的邀请。Paz Arés Osset提醒我们，我们是一体的，所有的人类都有着相同的追求，那就是和平与幸福。她的祈祷呼吁世界和平，所有来自不同精神道路的生物的统一，这样我们可以一起达到幸福，并从痛苦中解脱出来，被爱在其统一中所包围。
