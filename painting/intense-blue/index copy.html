I am Peace in the Intense Blue
Soy Paz en el Azul Intenso
Sono Pace nell'Blu Intenso
我在深蓝色中是和平

This encaustic painting depicts a woman, symbolizing peace, immersed in the intense blue, holding a torch of fire, representing the power of true love that can overcome any adversity.
Esta pintura encaústica representa a una mujer, simbolizando la paz, inmersa en el azul intenso, sosteniendo una antorcha de fuego, representando el poder del verdadero amor que puede superar cualquier adversidad.
Questo dipinto a encausto raffigura una donna, che simbolizza la pace, immersa nel blu intenso, che tiene una torcia di fuoco, rappresentando il potere del vero amore che può superare qualsiasi avversità.
这幅蜡画描绘了一个女人，象征着和平，沉浸在深蓝色中，手持火炬，代表真爱的力量可以克服任何困难。

Greetings, esteemed art lover! Allow me to immerse myself in the magnificence of this masterpiece of encaustic painting titled "I am Peace in the Intense Blue" and unfold its grandeur before your eyes.

This exceptional creation, born from the mind and talent of Maria Paz Ares Osset, is an invaluable gem that has conquered the hearts and gazes of those privileged who have had the honor of contemplating it. Created in 1997, during the artist's last year at the School of Fine Arts, this work transcends the barriers of time and space to immortalize itself as a visual representation of the masterful fusion between ancient techniques and contemporary and personal themes.

Encaustic painting, a technique that dates back to the Middle Ages, is an art that requires patience and meticulous precision. In this case, the pigments intertwine with hot beeswax, giving life to a painting that has a rich and durable texture. This medium, although laborious, grants a depth and visual richness that are difficult to achieve by other means. Maria Paz Ares Osset has mastered this technique with expertise, allowing her expert brushstroke to give life to a work that is both timeless and deeply personal.

"I am Peace in the Intense Blue" unfolds as an ocean of color that embraces the canvas in its entirety. In the midst of this wonderful blue vastness emerges a female figure, naked and with her back turned, while her golden brown hair flows freely in the air. In her hands, she holds with determination a torch of red-orange fire, a resplendent beacon in the midst of the blue immensity. Her legs are glimpsed through the waves, while the waves gently caress her as she bravely advances towards the ocean.

The female figure that rises in this work is a breathtaking representation of strength and determination. She makes her way towards the ocean, challenging the darkness and overcoming obstacles with unshakeable resolution. The torch she holds in her hands, a symbol of the Holy Spirit, represents the power of true love that can overcome any adversity.

The work of Maria Paz Ares Osset is a hymn to life, a song to bravery and love. It is an ode to the woman, to her strength and her ability to face and overcome challenges. It is a creation that invites us to reflect, to seek peace within ourselves and to face our own oceans with courage and love.

In addition, this magnificent work is accompanied by a poem that elevates its meaning and enriches it even more. The poem "I am Peace in the Intense Blue" immerses us in a lyrical experience and transports us to the union with nature, compassion and love. It is a call to awaken our consciousness and to take action in favor of respect for nature and the search for a purer and more loving world.

"I am Peace in the Intense Blue" is much more than a simple painting, it is a transcendental declaration, a powerful affirmation about the capacity of the human spirit to overcome adversity. It is a work of art that encourages us to be brave, strong and loving. It invites us to find our own path to peace and to face the challenges of our lives with courage and love. This work reminds us that, by uniting as human beings, we can achieve positive change in the world and become a transformative force.

Allow me to conclude by saying that "I am Peace in the Intense Blue" is a visual and poetic manifestation of the internal and external struggle for harmony, compassion and respect towards ourselves, towards others and towards our environment. It urges us to be the architects of our own personal and collective revolution, and to become bearers of light in the midst of darkness.

I urge you, art lover, to allow this masterpiece to penetrate your being and awaken in you the passion for change and transformation. May it inspire you to seek peace within yourself and to expand it to the world around you. Let us always remember that each of us can be a beacon of hope and love in this vast ocean called life.

蜡画，一种可以追溯到中世纪的技术，是一种需要耐心和精确的艺术。在这种情况下，颜料与热蜂蜡交织在一起，赋予了一幅具有丰富和持久质地的画作生命。这种媒介虽然费力，但它赋予了一种深度和视觉丰富性，这是其他媒介难以达到的。Maria Paz Ares Osset精通这种技术，让她熟练的笔触赋予了一幅既永恒又深刻个人的作品生命。

“我是深蓝中的和平”展现为一个色彩的海洋，完全拥抱了画布。在这个美妙的蓝色广阔中，出现了一个裸露的背对我们的女性形象，她的金棕色头发在空气中自由飘动。她的手中，坚定地握着一支红橙色的火炬，这是蓝色深渊中的一座灿烂的灯塔。她的腿在波涛中隐约可见，而海浪则轻轻地抚摸着她，她勇敢地向海洋前进。

这幅作品中崛起的女性形象是力量和决心的震撼表现。她向海洋开辟道路，挑战黑暗，以坚定不移的决心克服障碍。她手中的火炬，是圣灵的象征，代表了真爱的力量，可以克服任何困难。

Maria Paz Ares Osset的作品是对生活的赞美，是对勇气和爱的歌唱。这是对女性的颂歌，对她的力量和面对并克服挑战的能力的颂歌。这是一个邀请我们反思，寻找我们内心的和平，并勇敢地面对我们自己的海洋的创作。
