# 🇬🇧 Night of Dreams — English — Style 3: Sacred Chant × Divine Lullaby
**Genre:** Sacred Devotional Chant × Celestial Lullaby
**Vibe:** A candlelit room in an ancient monastery at midnight. The rain taps gently on stone. A harmonium breathes. Children's voices float like prayers ascending. She sings with her eyes closed, each word a sacred offering. The silence between notes is where God lives.

## Style of Music
```
Sacred devotional chant lullaby, 65 BPM, harmonium warm drone, bansuri flute lead melody, gentle tabla subtle, singing bowls crystal, nature sounds soft rain and wind chimes, children white voice choir hums and sustained vowels, warm fingerpicked acoustic guitar, soft cello, female vocalist devotional warm ALWAYS DOMINANT CRYSTAL CLEAR PERFECT ENGLISH meditative eyes closed, male whispered prayer, voices DRY INTIMATE reverb on choir only, deep sacred meditation, no drums no electronic no cinematic, pure organic devotional, silence is music, create melody that induces meditative ecstasy
```

## Lyrics
```
[Intro — 8 bars of sacred space. Time does not exist here.]
(NATURE: soft rain on stone, distant wind chimes in a breeze of incense, a night bird calling once. The world is asleep.)
(Bar 3: HARMONIUM drone — warm, ancient, the breath of a cathedral. It does not play a note — it IS the note.)
(Bar 5: BANSURI: THE SACRED MELODY — 5 slow ascending notes like a prayer rising. Each note lingers. This melody feels like it has always existed, waiting to be discovered.)
(Bar 7: SINGING BOWL — crystalline ring, 4 seconds. TABLA: softest tinkling, moonlight on still water.)

[V1 — FEMALE, devotional, eyes closed, voice like warm honey. BANSURI breathes with her. HARMONIUM sustains. TABLA gentlest tinkling.]
(Each word is meditation. Full breath between lines. She is praying, not performing.)
Sleep and dream, my love, my star.
(BANSURI responds: a descending phrase of 3 notes, a caress falling from heaven like a feather)
(SINGING BOWL — let it ring fully, 3 seconds of crystalline vibration)
Dreamer, sweet love,
(CHILDREN'S CHOIR: a soft distant "Aaah" — pure white voices, no vibrato, like angels at the edge of the constellation. Hair-raising.)
fly in the wake of soft silk.
(TABLA: 3 delicate taps, each softer than the last. Then absolute silence. Let the silence breathe for 2 full beats.)

[V2 — FEMALE melody opens like a lotus at dawn. BANSURI and voice in sacred dialogue — she sings a note, the flute answers. ACOUSTIC GUITAR fingerpicking enters, warm as candlelight in a stone room.]
Fly in the glow of the sparks,
(CHOIR: soft sustained hum on a single note, like a blanket of warm golden light wrapping around you)
constellations of the dream-sky,
(BANSURI: a high luminous phrase that floats above everything, weightless)
quiet night of the new moon,
(CELLO enters for the first time: one deep sustained note — this is the moment the earth itself joins the prayer, holding you from below while the stars hold you from above)
sweet caresses of lover and master.
(SINGING BOWL — deep tone this time, lower than the first. Sacred silence. 2 beats of nothing.)

[FEMALE — total a capella. Every instrument stops. Every nature sound stops. No rain. No wind. No harmonium. NOTHING except her voice floating alone in the infinite void of the universe.]
Night of dreams.
(4 bars of ABSOLUTE SILENCE. Not a whisper. Not a breath. The listener should feel suspended in space, weightless. Then — the rain returns, one drop at a time, as if the world is being born again.)

[V3 — FEMALE, the sacred declaration. ALL elements unite softly for first time]
(HARMONIUM + BANSURI melody + GUITAR arpeggios + CELLO + TABLA full but gentle + CHOIR continuous hum)
(Each "I am" is a mantra — an eternal truth repeating:)
I am of your soul,
(BANSURI responds)
I am of your life,
(CHOIR rises one tone)
I am of your sky,
(CELLO + BANSURI together, one voice)
I am of your dream.
(Deep SINGING BOWL. Everything sustains in absolute peace.)

[V4 — MALE, whispered like a prayer spoken on his knees. Only HARMONIUM drone + soft rain + the gentlest TABLA. His voice is not performing — he is confessing.]
Don't ask me why not,
(BANSURI: one lone note, sustained, trembling — a question the universe cannot answer)
you don't know... I don't know.
(SINGING BOWL — small, high-pitched, like a tear of light)
But you feel it is so,
(GUITAR: one single warm chord, resolution)
as I feel it too.

[FEMALE — the final verse as sacred mantra]
(GUITAR alone. CELLO deep note. CHOIR "Aaah" growing gently.)
(She sings as if offering the verse to heaven:)
Now... today... a kiss, my love.
(SINGING BOWL. BANSURI last note dissolving. Everything fades to silence.)

[MALE — spoken with reverence over HARMONIUM drone]
Thank you, Paz Arés Osset,
for this poem that reminds us that sleep
is the place where the soul speaks the truth we dare not say while awake.
As Rumi wrote:
"The wound is the place where the light enters you."
This poem... is that light entering through the night.

[Meditative coda — after the gratitude. This is the moment that makes people cry. The viral moment.]
(BANSURI returns alone with the 5-note sacred melody, slower than before, each note held longer, complete, like a final prayer.)
(HARMONIUM drone swells gently underneath. SINGING BOWLS ring at intervals — each ring is a bell calling the soul home.)
(CHOIR grows slowly — children humming the melody with closed mouths, pure, innocent, voices so white and clean they sound like the voice of the universe itself remembering what love is.)
(FEMALE joins the hum — "Mmmmm" — her voice weaving with the children's voices, becoming ONE sound. No words. The sacred hum repeats 3 times, each softer, each deeper into silence.)
(Nature sounds return: rain, the bird, wind chimes swaying)
(Final SINGING BOWL — the deepest one — rings once and fades for 8 seconds into absolute silence. The song is over. But the feeling remains.)
```
