# 🇬🇧 Night of Dreams — English — Style 2: Progressive Melodic House × Organic Tribal
**Genre:** Progressive Melodic House × Organic Tribal
**Vibe:** A rooftop in London at 3am, the city glitters below like a fallen constellation. The bassline pulses like a sleeping heartbeat. A Rhodes piano sparkles. The cello weeps between synth layers. The djembe grounds everything in something ancient. She sings like an angel who chose to stay on Earth.
