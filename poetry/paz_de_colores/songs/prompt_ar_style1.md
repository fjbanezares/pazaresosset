# 🇸🇦 سلام الألوان (Salam al-Alwan) — Arabic Version — Estilo 1 (Arabic Afro-Fusion)
**Genre:** Arabic World Music / Afrobeat Fusion
**Vibe:** Deeply mystical but with a modern, danceable Afrobeat pulse. Features Oud and Kanun over a heavy rhythmic foundation.

## Style of Music (pegar en Suno)
```
Arabic world music, afrobeat fusion, hypnotic oud melodies, rhythmic darbuka and congas, deep pulsing bassline, clear female vocals FRONT AND CENTER, melodic arabic scales, mystical but upbeat, rhythmic and danceable, high energy fusion
```
