# 🇦🇪 Salam al-Alwan (Paz de Colores) — Arabic Version — Estilo 2 (Calm Melodic House)
**Genre:** Calm Melodic House / Elegant Superproduction
**Vibe:** An exquisite melodic house superproduction. Deep, calm, but incredibly lush. Warm analog synths, an enveloping bass, and breathtaking atmospheres. Euphoric hands-in-the-air moments that rise from pure emotion and beauty, not from aggressive beats. Arabic singing with a modern electronic twist. The vocals are warm, crystal clear, and dominant.
