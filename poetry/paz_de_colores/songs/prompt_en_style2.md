# 🇬🇧 Peace of Colors (Paz de Colores) — English Version — Estilo 2 (Calm Melodic House)
**Genre:** Calm Melodic House / Elegant Superproduction
**Vibe:** An exquisite melodic house superproduction. Deep, calm, but incredibly lush. Warm analog synths, an enveloping bass, and breathtaking atmospheres. Euphoric hands-in-the-air moments that rise from pure emotion and beauty, not from aggressive beats. The vocals are warm, crystal clear, and dominant. Since the poem is short, phrases are repeated with ascending dynamics, and an original poetic bridge has been added to give the song full journey.
