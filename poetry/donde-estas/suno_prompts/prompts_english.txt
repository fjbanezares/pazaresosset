1. ENGLISH - STYLE A (Dramatic Acoustic Ballad - Harp & Cello)
-------------------------------------------------------------------

=== "Style of Music" FIELD IN SUNO: ===

dramatic acoustic ballad, emotional female vocals front and center, crystal clear diction, solo harp, cello, orchestral strings, cinematic, 70 BPM, no noise, deeply melancholic, powerful climax, pure clarity

=== "Lyrics" FIELD IN SUNO: ===

[Intro: solo harp arpeggios, slow and haunting, 4 bars]

[Verse 1: solo female voice over harp, extremely intimate]
In the golden afternoon...
where are you?... where are you?...
[cello enters with a long mournful note]
In the silver night...
when I want to love you...

[Verse 2: voice desperate, cello continues]
Where are you now...
child of my soul...
that I cannot find you?...
[strings enter softly]
Searching for you... among beautiful statues...

[Pre-Chorus: harp and cello weave, voice rises]
I search... I search for you...
Enamored face... smiling face...
Where are you... where are you?...

[Chorus: full strings swell, harp shimmers, voice powerful]
Strange happiness... of life and death...
extreme need... to feel your presence...
Crying inside... your unbearable absence...
There is no harder reality...
in this contradictory incongruence...

[Interlude: harp and cello melody, 4 bars]

[Verse 3: stripped to voice and cello, whispered pain]
Surrounded by brilliance... you notice nothing...
The pressure on my temples... contains all the weeping...
And just as in water... tears cannot be seen...
the mirror does not reflect... the tortured soul...

[Verse 4: voice breaking, only harp]
Pretending I live... and I am tired...
I want to live... and it is not permitted...
to live like crystal... so hard and fragile...
to live with the possible transparency... of a contradictory being...

[Verse 5: strings return, voice raw]
And here I am...
hidden from life... which passes and passes...
What are you doing?... what are you waiting for... why don't you embrace it?...

[Verse 6: harp arpeggios rise, warmth]
And here I am...
waiting for your gaze...
longing for your smile...
dreaming of your fire of love...
of love that burns...

[Verse 7: voice alone, desperate question]
Where?...
Now that I need you...
in the sea of pain that envelops me...
for not being on your shore...
kissed by your sand...

[Verse 8: cello weeps, quiet]
Where?...
Tomorrow... when your happiness...
will not notice my weary soul...

[Bridge: massive emotional build, all instruments]
Leave everything and come now!...
make me fly... like the breath of air...
make me shine... like the beam of light...
[climax, harp glissando]
and hear the murmur of life...
make me love love...

[Outro: instruments fade, voice alone]
Make me love... love...

[Spoken Outro: absolute silence]
Thank you Maria Paz Arés Osset,
For this poem,
Because beyond all despair...
love always arrives.
