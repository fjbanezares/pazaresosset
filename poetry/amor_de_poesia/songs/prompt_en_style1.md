# 🇬🇧 Love of Poetry — English — Ambient Folk × Ethereal Trip-Hop
**Genre:** Ambient Folk × Ethereal Trip-Hop
**Vibe:** A rain-drenched London evening, condensation on a café window, the smell of old books. Think Portishead meets Iron & Wine — slow, hypnotic trip-hop beats wrapped in warm acoustic guitar fingerpicking and ethereal vocal harmonics. She sings with a voice that sounds like it's remembering something beautiful and painful at the same time. He speaks the verses like reading a passage from a diary he found in an antique shop. The trip-hop beat gives it that modern, cinematic weight — but the acoustic guitar keeps it human, intimate, honest. This is the sound of love dissolving into art.
