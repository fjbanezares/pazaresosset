# 🇬🇧 Love of Poetry — English — Style 2: Melodic Techno × Ecstatic Hymn to Creation
**Genre:** Melodic Techno × Ecstatic Hymn
**Vibe:** Berlin meets Burning Man at sunrise. The bass is a living organism — deep, relentless, driving. But above it, cascading arpeggios rise like prayers ascending to the sky. She is a priestess of sound — her voice cuts through walls of synth like sunlight through stained glass. He is the philosopher, the spoken word anchor who grounds the ecstasy in meaning. The poem's cyclical structure — "love of poetry" returning again and again — becomes a trance-inducing mantra that transforms grief into celebration. Every build is worship. Every drop is rebirth. This is music that makes you close your eyes, raise your hands, and surrender to the beauty of being alive.
