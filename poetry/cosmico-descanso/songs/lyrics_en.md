## ⚡ SONG 1: COSMIC ECSTASY (ELECTRONIC HANDS-IN-THE-AIR)
**Style:** [Style: Simple, effective Melodic Progressive House, ethereal atmospheric pads, steady deep thumping kick, 128 BPM, monumental hands-in-the-air moments, nostalgic but hopeful trance arpeggios, stadium building energy, joyful liberation, crystal-clear female recitation, hypnotic and transcendent, perfect English diction]

[Intro]
[Ambient space textures, distant stardust sparkles]
[Female Voice: Soft, intimate, clear English]
Light ballerina of aquamarine...
In circles of white tulle... divine.

[Verse 1]
[Simple electronic throb starts, very clean]
Crystalline beauty, so feminine
Resting, Venus-like, she reclines
Rest for the cosmic wanderer
Present at her feet behind the scenes

[Build-up]
[Snare roll begins, rising synth lead, high energy building]
To dance like a swan or a swallow
And shine...
And shine...
SHINE!
Like glitter, my dancer

[Drop]
[Explosive melodic peak, hands-in-the-air euphoria, bright synths]
(Cosmic Rest)
(Dancing with the stars)
(Celestial joy)

[Verse 2]
[Energy stays high but focused]
Paz Arés Osset... 2020...
Light ballerina of aquamarine
In circles of white tulle, divine.

[Outro]
[Music fades into a warm electronic wash]
[Female Voice: Deep gratitude]
Thank you Maria Paz Arés Osset for allowing us, through this poem, to express the ecstasy of cosmic rest and the beauty of the dance.
