# 🇬🇧 Poetry — English Version — Style 1 (Ethereal Dream Pop × Chillwave)
**Genre:** Ethereal Dream Pop × Chillwave
**Vibe:** A sun-drenched, dream-like state. Imagine a wide, open luminous space where every sound is a shimmer of light. The music is lush, with layers of shimmering guitars, soft analog synths, and a gentle, heartbeat-like electronic beat. The vocals are heavenly, soaring, and slightly reverberated, sounding like an angel singing from a distant star. It's the sound of a "divine spark" igniting into a beautiful melody.
