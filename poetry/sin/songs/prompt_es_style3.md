# Style of Music
Complex melodic house, Ibiza sunset anthem, euphoric hands in the air drop, interspersed with a sensual romantic acoustic bachata rhythm section, beautiful cello melodies combined with clean electric guitar licks, subtle tribal percussion djembes, nature sounds seagulls ocean waves jungle birds, highly melodic female vocals ALWAYS IN FRONT, emotionally intense, uplifting dance, crystal clear Spanish pronunciation

# Lyrics
[Intro - Ibiza Sunset]
(Ocean waves crashing gently. Distant seagulls and jungle birds)
(A warm tribal house groove fades in with subtle djembes)
(A melancholic cello plays a beautiful sweeping melody)
(Melodic female vocals, intimate but rhythmic)
Sin a-mor... no hay vi-da.
Sin vi-da... la muer-te.

[Verse 1 - Building the groove]
(The house beat becomes tighter. Clean electric guitar adds subtle rhythmic licks)
Son-rí-e, fin-ge, son-rí-e.
Sin amor, sonríe, finge.
Sonríe niña... no a-mes.

[Pre-Chorus - Rising energy]
(Tribal drums increase intensity. Cello swells up)
(Vocals soar, powerful and melodic)

Su-su-rros, cuidado, no seas mala, no ames.
Sincera no, finge máscara de buena niña.
Si-len-cio, no escribas versos, calla, no hables.

[Breakdown - Nature returns]
(The beat drops out completely. Only jungle birds and a lone electric guitar playing softly)
(Vocals return to an intimate, empty whisper)

Sin pena, ni gloria, cómoda pasa y
siem-pre un precio se pa-ga.

[The Sensual Bachata Awakening - 16 bars]
(A beautiful, romantic, and highly rhythmic Dominican Bachata suddenly takes over)
(A bright acoustic requinto guitar plays a complex, syncopated, deeply emotional melody)
(The bass plays a warm, syncopated bachata rhythm: boom... boom-boom. Bongos and güira enter)
(Vocals become extremely sensual, intimate, whispered close to the ear, dancing to the rhythm)
So-le-dad en com-pa-ñí-a...
Sentimientos gol-pe-a-dos.
Su-frir hasta amar, sufrir,
sufrir por amor, llo-rar.

[The Fusion Build-up]
(Slowly, the massive melodic house synthesizers start creeping back in beneath the bachata rhythm)
(Tribal drums roll rapidly over the güira. Cello and electric guitar play a fast, ascending harmony)
(Vocals belt out a powerful, long note)
Sen-tir el amor... vi-vir.

[Climax - The Ultimate Fusion Drop!]
(Massive euphoric melodic house drop fused with the bachata bassline! Total euphoria, Ibiza sunset vibes)
(Thick bassline, soaring cellos, and electric guitar leads over bachata percussion)
(Vocals reach maximum intensity, joyful and liberated)
Sa-ber es-pe-rar... a-mar.
Ser só-lo ser a-mor.
Ser vi-da y sol fe-liz.

[Outro]
(The house beat fades slowly into ocean waves and seagulls)
(A final beautiful cello note sustains and fades)
