# Style of Music
Complex melodic house, Ibiza sunset anthem, euphoric hands in the air drop, interspersed with a sensual romantic acoustic bachata rhythm section, beautiful cello melodies combined with clean electric guitar licks, subtle tribal percussion djembes, nature sounds seagulls ocean waves jungle birds, highly melodic female vocals ALWAYS IN FRONT, emotionally intense, uplifting dance, crystal clear English pronunciation
