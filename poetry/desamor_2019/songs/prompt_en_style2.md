# 🇬🇧 Heartbreak (2019) — English Version — Estilo 2 (Organic Electronic / Afro-Piano)
**Genre:** Organic Electronic / Afro-Cinematic House
**Vibe:** Elegant electronic tapestry with live African percussion (djembés, shakers, congas), an emotional grand piano as the melodic anchor, haunting cello, and tasteful analog synths. The vocal is EVERYTHING: pure, clear, beautifully enunciated English, always towering above the mix. Multiple euphoric "hands in the air" catharsis moments. No cheap beats — only grace.

## Style of Music (pegar en Suno)
```
Organic electronic, cinematic afro house, live djembe and conga percussion, acoustic wood shakers, emotional grand piano arpeggios, haunting cello melody, warm analog synthesizer pads, lush atmospheric reverb, beautiful clear female vocals PROMINENTLY IN FRONT, crystal clear English pronunciation, intimate storytelling delivery, euphoric hands-in-the-air buildup moments, elegant and refined, no aggressive beats, high fidelity studio production
```
