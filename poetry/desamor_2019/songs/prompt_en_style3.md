# 🇬🇧 Heartbreak (2019) — English Version — Style 3 (Trance House 128 BPM)
**Genre:** Emotional Trance / Progressive House — 128 BPM
**Vibe:** Elegant trance house with discrete djembe underneath, cinematic piano, deep cello, and warm analog synths. The VOICE is everything: warm, beautiful, perfectly enunciated English, always dominant in the mix. FULL POEM included. Numbers written as words so Suno pronounces them correctly. Syllable-separated vocalization at key dramatic moments for maximum impact.
