# 🇪🇸 Desamor (2019) — Versión Español — Estilo 1 (Neoclassical Emotional Piano)
**Género:** Neoclassical / Emotional Cinematic
**Vibe:** Profunda tristeza que evoluciona hacia una tensión dramática y termina en una paz melancólica. Un piano protagonista que narra la historia.

## Style of Music (pegar en Suno)
```
Neoclassical piano, emotional cinematic strings, melancholic cello solo, sudden dramatic crescendo, heavy piano chords for aggression, ethereal atmospheric background, clear female vocals FRONT AND CENTER, vulnerable and raw delivery, intense emotional buildup, high fidelity, inspire yourself also with some worship to god sounds
```
