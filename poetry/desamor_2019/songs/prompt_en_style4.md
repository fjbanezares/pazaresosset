# 🇬🇧 Heartbreak (2019) — English Version — Style 4 (Ibiza Sunset Deep House)
**Genre:** Balearic Deep House / Melodic Vocal House
**Vibe:** Imagine the sun setting over Cala Conta, Ibiza — golden light, warm breeze, bare feet in the sand. A shimmering deep house groove lifts the whole poem into something you can *feel* in your body. The melody is the hook — a gorgeous, singable Rhodes and piano chord progression that gets stuck in your head. Soft tropical percussion and a bouncy, warm bass keep it moving. The VOICE is radiant, sunny, crystal clear — she's telling you a painful story but she's already on the other side, stronger, dancing. No darkness, no cinematic weight. Just light, melody, and truth.

## Lyrics (paste in Suno)
```
[Intro - 8 bars, Rhodes melody plays the hook, shuffle beat drops in, warm bass]
(NO vocals — just the vibe, summer air, breezy and inviting)

[Verse 1 - Voice enters over Rhodes and soft beat, breezy and warm]
(Clear warm voice, intimate but with a smile, DOMINANT in the mix)
A recurring memory,
wakes me every night,
with a silent scream.

[Verse 2 - Piano joins, bongos enter softly, bass groove deepens]
(Storytelling, natural, like talking to a friend on the beach)
It was the fourteenth of December, two thousand and fifteen,
I woke up as always...
in love,
and in your discouragement,
I spoke words of encouragement:

[Pre-Chorus - Rhodes melody lifts, filtered arpeggio shimmers]
(Voice tender and hopeful, singing it like a melody)
"You are rich"...
No, you denied, "I am poor",
I explained...
"you are rich... in my great love."

[Chorus - Bass drops deeper, tambourine lifts, MELODIC DROP, feel-good energy]
(Voice strong and beautiful, riding the groove, HANDS IN THE AIR moment)
No! No! No!
You got angry,
and you lifted the bed,
and you threw the bed at me,
and I was falling from the castle we created!

[Break - 4 bars, Rhodes melody solo with filtered delay, dreamy]

[Verse 3 - Beat drops to half, voice becomes intimate]
(Soft, close, like a whisper on a warm night)
I stood... pe-tri-fied... and silent,
hearing your screams
of fierce attack.
Seeing your eyes of hate,
seeing your strained neck,
seeing your teeth... screaming.

[Bridge - Piano and pads, spiritual warmth, golden hour energy]
(Voice floating, ethereal but perfectly clear)
I entered into prayer, I observed you,
I didn't understand what was happening...
In a divided peace... I for-gave you.

[Verse 4 - Groove returns, bongos back, building naturally]
(Voice gaining confidence, free and warm)
I stayed between the bed and the wall,
the upright bed was my protective shield.
You grew tired of screaming,
you left and abandoned me,
and didn't even apologize.

[Build - Synth filter opens, arpeggio rises, percussion fills]
(Voice growing with the warmth, clear above everything)
A year I spent waiting,
giving you the greatest love,
your soul for me was absent...

I kept enduring the sorrow,
I wanted to understand,
I wanted to rebuild your love.

[Drop - Full groove, maximum melodic energy, IBIZA SUNSET MOMENT]
(Voice radiant, free, dancing with the music)
More than a year passed,
and June arrived,
I said goodbye to you.
You threatened:
"We won't be friends"...

[Breakdown - Just Rhodes chords and voice, intimate again]
(Beautiful honest voice, no tears, just truth)
Afterwards, you didn't even call me...
nor did you greet me...
ab-sent...
you didn't even respect me.

[Final - Bass and beat return one last time, then fade to Rhodes and voice]
(Voice clear and powerful, the revelation, she's free now)
Rea-li-zing
your lack... of love...

was the saddest thing!
And I fi-nal-ly understood
that I was not love,
I was... whatever you felt like.

[Outro - Rhodes melody plays the hook one last time, beat fades, waves]
(Let the melody breathe, 8 bars, sunset)

[Spoken - Warm voice over last Rhodes chord]
Thank you, María Paz Arés Osset,
for turning heartbreak into a dance under the sun.
As Saint Augustine said:
"The measure of love is to love without measure."
```
