# 🇮🇹 Disamore (2019) — Italian Version — Estilo 1 (Symphonic Rock Ballad)
**Genre:** Symphonic Rock / Dramatic Italian Ballad
**Vibe:** Operatic, grand, and intensely emotional. Think Italian classic drama with modern rock elements for the aggression parts.

Symphonic rock ballad, dramatic italian opera elements, heavy electric guitar accents, soaring orchestral strings, clear female vocals FRONT AND CENTER, passionate and grand, intense emotional delivery, rhythmic and theatrical, high fidelity
