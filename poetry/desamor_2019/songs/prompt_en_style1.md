# 🇬🇧 Heartbreak (2019) — English Version — Estilo 1 (Dark Ambient / Cello)
**Genre:** Dark Ambient / Orchestral Ballad
**Vibe:** Heavy, atmospheric, and cinematic. Focuses on the "shield" and the "waiting" through a deep, pulsing rhythm.

## Style of Music (pegar en Suno)
```
Dark ambient atmosphere, deep orchestral cello, rhythmic heartbeat pulse, cinematic tension, clear female vocals FRONT AND CENTER, emotional and heavy, dramatic strings, reverb-heavy piano, high production value, hauntingly beautiful
```
