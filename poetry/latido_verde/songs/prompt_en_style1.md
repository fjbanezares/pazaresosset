# 🇬🇧 Green Heartbeat — English Version — Style 1 (Uplifting Indie Folk)
**Genre:** Indie Folk / Acoustic Pop
**Vibe:** Walking barefoot through a sun-dappled forest, guitar on your back. Bright acoustic guitar, gentle banjo picking, soft tambourine, warm harmonies. The voice is radiant, clear, like someone who's found something sacred in a tree and wants you to feel it too. An uplifting anthem for nature — the kind of song you sing around a campfire. No heaviness, just light and truth.

## Lyrics (paste in Suno)
```
[Intro - 8 bars, acoustic guitar picking, birds chirping, tambourine enters]
(NO vocals — golden forest atmosphere)

[Verse 1 - Voice enters alone with guitar, warm and clear]
(Warm radiant voice, perfect pronunciation, DOMINANT in the mix)
Through forests and gardens,
my spirit contemplates and feels,
I embrace each tree,
sensing its la-tent bea-ting.

In every leaf that falls,
in every branch that sways,
I hear the mystic whisper
that makes my soul... tremble.

[Verse 2 - Banjo joins, tambourine, groove starts to build]
(Voice hopeful, like inviting the world to join in)
With hope I join the poets,
souls who rhyme with love,
together we raise our voice,
in a pro-tec-tive echo.

We sing about fruit trees,
of dazzling colors,
about birds that fly above,
with songs that ne-ver fade.

[Chorus - Full band, handclaps, anthemic, HANDS IN THE AIR]
(Voice powerful and free, singing like a declaration of love to nature)
I summon artists,
musicians and actors of renown,
join this song,
let it resound in every soul!

Because felling without reason,
just to build without end,
is to for-get the essence,
is to stop... brea-thing.

[Bridge - Just guitar and voice, spiritual, reverent]
(Voice soft and reverent, like a prayer to the earth)
The trees give us life,
shade, shelter and nourishment,
their importance is vital,
in this and every moment.

With poems and art,
I want to teach everyone,
the urgency of saving trees,
and em-bra-cing Mo-ther Earth.

[Chorus 2 - Maximum energy, joyful, luminous]
(Voice soaring, the most beautiful verse of the whole poem)
Because in every birdsong and fruit,
in every leaf that sways,
there is a pro-found message
that makes the heart... tremble.

Let us love the trees,
let us tend them with devotion,
for in their well-being,
we find our sal-va-tion.

[Verse 3 - Intimate return, just guitar and voice]
(Tender voice, like making a sacred promise)
In a silent pact,
I approach each tree,
I embrace its essence,
I feel its inner pulse.

With them I exchange energy,
hope and love,
for a world in harmony,
without war or pain.

[Outro - Voice free, almost spoken, guitar fading]
(Intimate, personal, like a diary entry)
After writing the poem,
I go to embrace all the trees,
from now on... to greet them,
and exchange energy.

[Spoken - Whisper over last guitar note]
Thank you, María Paz Arés Osset,
for reminding us that to hug a tree
is to hug life itself.
As Saint Francis of Assisi said:
"Start by doing what is necessary, then what is possible,
and suddenly you are doing the impossible."
```
