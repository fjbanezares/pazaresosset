# 🇬🇧 Infinite Love — English Version — Melodic Style

> **Why this style?** For the English version, we aim for a contemporary worship-influenced cinematic ballad. English lends itself beautifully to soaring melodies with clear, emotive diction. The orchestral backdrop with ethereal synth layers creates a transcendent, universal spiritual experience.
