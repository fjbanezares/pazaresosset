# 🇬🇧 Infinite Love — English Version — Euphoric Dance Style

> **Why this style?** The first style was cinematic and worshipful. This second style is a Euphoric Spiritual Dance/Gospel House track. It completely shifts the mood to pure celebration and joy. It is extremely rhythmic and uplifting, representing the "Dance with ecstasy" line perfectly.

## Style of Music (paste in Suno)
```
euphoric spiritual house, joyful dance pop, uplifting piano chords, powerful soul female vocals, driving four-on-the-floor beat, gospel choir accents, celebratory, energetic and bright
```
