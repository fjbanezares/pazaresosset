# 🇬🇧 Optimism and Positivity — English — Spoken Word × UK Garage House
**Genre:** Spoken Word Poetry × UK Garage Deep House
**Vibe:** Rooftop at golden hour, London meets Ibiza. The British spoken word tradition is poetry at its most alive — measured cadence, emotional weight in every syllable. She sings the light with a voice like sunrise through stained glass. The production is warm UK garage: shuffled beats, deep sub-bass glow, warm Rhodes chords. Not dark — GOLDEN. Every word is a declaration of intent against fear. The battle here is internal: darkness vs. the choice to shine.

## Lyrics (paste in Suno)
```
[Intro - 8 bars, warm Rhodes, deep bass pulse, tambourine, golden light atmosphere]
(NO vocals — sunrise rooftop atmosphere, warm and inviting)

[Verse 1 - FEMALE, radiant, like announcing a new day]
(Beautiful golden voice, soaring, clear pronunciation, DOMINANT)
On horizons of light and of hope,
where posi-ti-vi-ty blooms,
a poem rises with confidence,
singing to optimism and its truth.

[Verse 2 - MALE, spoken word, warm and measured]
(Warm slam poet flow, like reading truth at sunrise, gentle conviction)
Let the sun gild every morning,
illuminating the soul with its brilliance,
let our smile be our great feat,
and in an embrace... let the world be simpler.

[Build - Rhodes ascending, bass deepens, tambourine intensifies]

[Chorus 1 - FEMALE, hopeful, soaring above the beat]
(Eyes closed, hands rising, emotional crescendo)
Let the seed of joy sprout,
in every heart that dares to try,
and let optimism, strong, rescue us...

[MALE enters underneath, spoken word counterpoint]
...from the SHA-DOWS, guiding us to the heights.

[Drop 1 - BOTH TOGETHER, synth explodes, euphoric, HANDS IN THE AIR]
(Both voices layered, celebratory, the biggest moment)
Po-si-ti-vi-ty is the ban-ner!
That in our soul, we proudly carry!
And with every dawn, the spring-time
of the heart, together we... ce-le-brate!

[Break - 4 bars, just Rhodes piano and bass pulsing, breathe]

[Verse 3 - MALE, spoken word, vulnerable but strong]
(Music drops, confession, honest and raw)
Life sometimes strikes us down,
but let us not let fear defeat us...

[FEMALE completes, whisper growing into light]
(Enters like sunlight breaking through clouds)
...because the light, within us, is re-born,
and optimism is our great re-me-dy.

[Build Final - Everything rises, synths opening like a sunrise]

[Chorus Final - ALTERNATING, one line each, maximum beauty]
(Call and response, intimate and powerful)

[MALE - with inner fire]
So then, may hope never be absent,

[FEMALE - luminous]
nor the fire of optimism in our lives,

[MALE - with conviction, almost joyful shout]
for they are the force that launches us...

[FEMALE - closing with full voice, glorious]
...to conquer goals NE-VER before a-chieved.

[Outro - Beat lifts upward, doesn't fade down. FEMALE hums melody]
(The song rises and dissolves upward, like the sun climbing)

[Spoken - MALE, warm grateful voice, over final reverb]
Thank you, Paz Arés Osset,
for gifting us a sunrise in verse,
for reminding us that optimism
is not denying the night
but choosing to light the flame.
As Maya Angelou once wrote:
"There is no greater agony than bearing
an untold story inside you."
This poem... is the story she chose to tell.
```
