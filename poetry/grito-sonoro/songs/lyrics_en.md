# Resounding Cry - English Sessions (Full Poem)

---

## ⚡ SONG 1: THE UNIVERSAL ANTHEM (ELECTRONIC/GOSPEL)
**Style:** [Style: Spiritual, euphoric, cinematic electronic masterpiece, Orchestral Progressive House, Gospel choir, uplifting Trance, monumental production, explosive joyous liberation, stadium anthem, historical good vibes, crystal-clear female recitation, perfect English diction, hands-in-the-air ecstasy]

[Intro]
[Atmospheric synth pads, single resonant piano note]
[Female Recitation: Whisper, intimate, crystal clear]
Now...
Now is the time for the resounding cry.
Now...
Now is the time for the cry.
Now is the time for the resounding cry!

[Verse 1]
[Soft orchestral strings enter, building warmth]
They want us silent
Submissive,
Submerged in soundless cries
Soundless, soundless ...
shhss, shss
hush, hush

[Verse 2]
[Progressive House pulse begins, 128 BPM]
Who transformed us into dolls?
Who into images and mannequins?
Who made us servants?
Who made us objects of desire?

[Pre-Chorus]
[Rising energy, rhythmic strings]
Criticized,
Judged,
Ignored,
Disregarded,
and even humiliated,
Raped and murdered.

[Bridge]
[Beat drops out, soulful Gospel harmony]
Where were the values lost?
Where is the ethics?
Where is true love?

[Verse 3]
[Intense build-up, heavy kicks]
If we have been victims of executioners
Of wolves in sheep's clothing,
Manipulators,
Beliefs,
Prejudices,
Sexist systems …
Victims even of ourselves!

[Chorus]
[Explosive drop, massive melodic trance energy]
Now! Now that we are fed up!
Fed up with silent cries
Now is the time for the resounding cry
Now is the time for My resounding cry!
A resounding cry, like a batucada,
A feminine and feminist cry
To balance the scales
In equality and freedom

[Verse 4]
[High energy continues, triumphant trumpets]
And how can I, who love the creative silence,
Reach a deafening cry?
With a heart full of love
I will create this innovation
In my candor.

[Verse 5]
[Celestial and grand]
Woman
Divine being,
Your mere presence
Is a work of art

[Crescendo]
[Historical anthem feel, maximum intensity]
I cry for the women!
Women, cry out!
Now is the time!
For those who die
For those who will die
For those who will be born
For Mother Nature
who also dies.

[Climax]
[Universal light energy]
In a resounding cry
In a sung cry
Expressed in a pictorial cry
Musical cry
Listen to my resounding cry!
Summary of millions of soundless cries!
Mine and all women's!
I cry:
Aaahhh ….!

[Outro]
[Serene piano and soft choir]
I hope it remains
Turned into
A perfume of
True love.

[Outro Dedication]
[Warm spoken voice over soft ethereal pad]
Thank you Maria Paz Ares Osset for allowing us, through this poem, to express the cry of women and all oppressed beings.

[End]
