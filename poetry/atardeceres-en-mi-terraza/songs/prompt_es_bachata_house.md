# Style of Music
Melodic Ibicenco House fused with Authentic Acoustic Bachata, 120 BPM. Atmospheric sunset soundscape with mountain breeze and birds. Elegant Spanish guitar and Bachata requinto melodies (syncopated picking) intertwining with deep analog pads. Rhythmic tribal percussion: congas, bongos, and guira creating a danceable Bachata-House groove. Deep pulsing bassline. Emotional, breathy, and dominant female vocals, crystal clear Spanish pronunciation. Cinematic arpeggios representing "sequins", ethereal reverse reverbs, and intentional silences for tension. Progressive emotional build-up from a serene acoustic intro to a rhythmic, euphoric dance climax. High-end production, organic and electronic fusion. Sensual, spiritual, and radiant.
