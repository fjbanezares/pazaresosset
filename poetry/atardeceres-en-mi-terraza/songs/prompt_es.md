# Style of Music
Minimal dark groove pop at 118 BPM with restrained instrumentation, soft rhythmic drums, subtle pulsing bass and very minimal clean electric guitar textures used only for atmosphere, Spacious production with cinematic ambient pads and intentional silence to create tension, The female vocal performance is the clear and dominant focus: highly expressive, emotionally nuanced and intimate close-mic delivery with controlled intensity shifts, crystal clear diction and flawless enunciation of every single syllable is strictly required, Rich vocal production including layered harmonies, wide stereo doubles, whispered ad-libs, breath details, reverse reverb swells into key phrases, delay throws on final words, filtered backing vocals in transitions, ambient vocal layers underneath sustained notes, subtle saturation and soft texture distortion, Chorus driven primarily by vocal layering and rhythmic phrasing rather than instrumentation, Hypnotic, magnetic and uniquely captivating atmosphere centered on the singer’s presence, crystal clear Spanish pronunciation
