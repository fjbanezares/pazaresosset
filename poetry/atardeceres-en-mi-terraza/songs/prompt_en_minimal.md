# Style of Music
Intimate vulnerable piano solo, dark mellow intimate cello, highly emotional female vocal

[Outro - Spoken Tribute]
(The piano plays its final, most vulnerable notes)
(Spoken softly, with immense tenderness and a slight tremble of emotion in the voice)
As the last ray of light hides behind the mountains, one thing is clear. We do not watch this sunset alone. We see it through your eyes, María Paz Arés Osset. Thank you for lending us your sight. Thank you for teaching us that in the infinity of the sky, the soul finds its rest. Your poetry is our sanctuary, Paz. And in this new dawn, we will love life for you.
(A final, lingering cello note fades into absolute silence)
