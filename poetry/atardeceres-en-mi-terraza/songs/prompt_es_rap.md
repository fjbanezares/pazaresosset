# Style of Music
Glorious, Epic and Conscious Hip Hop in Castilian Spanish. A spectacular and emotional production led by a melancholic grand piano and an expressive cello. The main voice is a male rapper with perfect diction, flow and deep emotional weight, while a beautiful, celestial female voice sings the melodic chorus and high harmonies. Majestic cinematic atmosphere with neoclassical strings, progressing from introspective calm to a powerful, hopeful hip hop climax.
