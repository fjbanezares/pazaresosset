# 🇺🇸 Flowery Easter — English Version — Style 1
**Genre:** Cinematic Spiritual Pop / Gospel Crossover
**Ideal for:** Global virality, Easter playlists, spiritual wellness audiences

## Style of Music (paste in Suno)
```
cinematic spiritual pop, gospel crossover, lead female vocals FRONT AND CENTER clear enunciation, lyrics intelligible above all instruments, soulful powerful voice, full orchestral strings, sacred percussion, ethereal backing choir (soft, not competing), emotional crescendo, vocals carry the entire song
```

[Intro - Instrumental]
(Soft piano, distant choir whispers, strings rising slowly)

Now… I am.

I feel my rhythm.
I feel the fluidity.
Fluidity of slow Movement
Arising by itself, without my action

(strings bloom, emotion rises visibly in the voice)
I enjoy releasing my being
My breath lives this moment
of Divine creation unfolding
Now I am prayer…

(music steps back, voice and one instrument only, intimate)
Breath is released

(clear enunciation, sacred drums enter)
Glimmers of Love
Glimmers of Peace

I am breath,
I feel Life
I feel the flow of breathing

[Climax]
(highest note of the entire song — pure, clear, glorious)
I am re-sur-rec-tion
I am in God

[Verse 6]

I feel that Eternal Present.
As Je-sus showed

He embraced the cross for us.
He rose. He ne-ver died.
His Present being Present.
Now eternally.
Present in my turning.
The tur-ning of Life.

[Outro]
(voice alone, tender and vast, like a breath)
Breathe in the gift of Life, Peace and Love

I feel your being…

Namaste
Namaskar

[Final - Spoken over fading strings]
(voice clear, warm, unhurried — a sacred closing)
Thank you, María Paz Arés Osset,
for reminding us that God can be reached through dance.
As Saint Augustine wrote:
"You have made us for Yourself,
and our hearts are restless
until they rest in You."
In your words, in your dance —
we find that rest.
Thank you for your light.

(Final chord dissolves into silence)
