# 🇬🇧 Horizon of Love — English — Style 4: Melodic Spoken Word Rap × Crystal Clear Recitation
**Genre:** Melodic Spoken Word Rap × Lo-Fi Cinematic
**Vibe:** Every single word must be UNDERSTOOD. This is not a song — it is a poem being PERFORMED over music. The beat is minimal: a warm lo-fi hip-hop groove with soft piano chords and a gentle bass. She recites with melodic rap flow — rhythmic, musical, but every syllable is crystal clear. He enters as a dialogue partner in key moments — his voice is deeper, warmer, answering her. The music NEVER overpowers the voice. Voices are ALWAYS front and center, dry and present. No heavy reverb. No drowning in synths. The poem is the star.

## Lyrics (paste in Suno)
```
[Intro - 4 bars, soft piano chord, lo-fi beat enters gently, warm bass]
(NO vocals — just the beat breathing, intimate and close)

[FEMALE — melodic recitation, warm and close, like telling a secret]
(Crystal clear, every word PERFECTLY pronounced, rhythmic but natural)
Surprise... of a night... in life,

[FEMALE — same tone, painting the scene]
(Slower, each word is a brushstroke)
in a forest... of shadow trees,

[FEMALE — voice warms, sensual]
(A little lower, the heat arrives)
summery... dark... and luminous.

[FEMALE — whisper becoming speech, seductive intent]
(Almost whispering, then landing on the last word with weight)
Hot. Exciting. Mysterious.

[Beat shifts slightly — snare enters softly]

[FEMALE — voice lifts, declarative, melodic rap flow kicks in]
(Rhythmic now, riding the beat, but CLEAR)
Night of fire,
enchanted night.

[FEMALE — listing emotions, each one a separate beat]
(Staccato delivery, punching each word gently)
Of sadness. Of joy.
Of fear. Bliss.

[FEMALE — the word lands alone, with weight]
(Full stop. The most important word. Let it ring.)
Love.

[Piano chord change — emotional shift]

[FEMALE — fragile, intimate, the tears arrive]
(Voice cracks slightly, vulnerability, still crystal clear)
Night of tears,
night of kisses,
of kisses and caresses... unexpected.

[MALE — enters for the first time, deep warm voice, mysterious]
(Like a narrator stepping out of shadow — measured, noble)
Enigmatic midnight.
Secret midnight.

[Beat opens — bass deepens, piano arpeggio]

[FEMALE — desire blooming, voice grows warmer and fuller]
(She's remembering something beautiful — the rhythm flows naturally)
Summer lap, joyful July,
you break my long-found sleep
with your burning... winged lips.

[FEMALE — conflict, push and pull, melodic rap]
(Faster rhythm, the internal battle, still perfectly clear)
I want your passion that comes as a gift,
I want... and I don't want,
fear of the untested flow.

[Beat drops to just bass and piano — TENSION]

[FEMALE — THE PLEA — the most important section — FULL VOICE, raw emotion]
(She stops rapping. She's SPEAKING now. Directly. To someone. To love itself.)
Oh! Horizon of love,

[FEMALE — each line is a command, desperate and beautiful]
(Measured, deliberate, each "don't" hits like a heartbeat)
Don't ever let me falter beside you.
Don't let me leave you.
Don't let me approach your edge.
Don't let me reach the precipice.

[FEMALE — voice breaks, the most vulnerable moment]
(Almost crying, raw, stripped bare, the truest line)
Please... hold me... very tight.
Let me not know... the brink of your death.

[2 bars silence — just heartbeat bass]

[FEMALE — surrender, soft melodic flow returns, silk voice]
(Calm now, decided, floating over the beat like silk)
Never again to live outside your vessel,
always to remain in your soft silk.
To vibrate each day in your breast alone.
Only to exist and feel your paradise... alone.

[MALE — enters with gravitas, a revelation]
(Deep, warm, like truth arriving)
True living.
Eternal living.

[FEMALE — responds, completing his thought]
(Simple, direct, no embellishment, the purest statement)
I no longer want to die.
I want to kiss your sign... alone.

[Piano becomes warmer — the final movement]

[FEMALE — the closing litany, each line a caress, melodic and tender]
(The softest voice of the entire piece — listing what love IS — each line a separate breath)
You are my desired night,
my soft and gentle voice,
my song of whispers,
my smiling moon,
my memory of heaven,

[FEMALE — the last line, full voice, the emotional peak]
(Everything she has — not loud, but FULL — the final truth)
the warmth... of my love.

[Outro — piano fades, beat dissolves, silence]

[MALE — final whisper, barely audible]
Thank you, Paz Arés Osset,
for this horizon.
As Mary Oliver wrote:
"Tell me, what is it you plan to do
with your one wild and precious life?"
This poem... is her answer.
```
