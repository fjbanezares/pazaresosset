# Style of Music
Deep organic house, very subtle pulsing beat, uplifting soul-elevating melodies, warm electric piano, euphoric hands in the air moments, beautiful clear female vocals ALWAYS IN FRONT AND DOMINANT, crystal clear Spanish pronunciation, emotional cinematic drops, exquisite acoustic classical guitar outro
