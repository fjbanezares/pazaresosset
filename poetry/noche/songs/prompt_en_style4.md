# Style of Music
Epic massive electronic dance music, high vibration trance, euphoric stadium house, interspersed with a sensual romantic acoustic bachata rhythm section in the middle, beautiful clear female vocals ALWAYS IN FRONT AND DOMINANT, crystal clear English pronunciation, majestic synthesizers, huge emotional drops, intricate bachata guitars and bongos, cinematic acoustic classical piano and guitar outro
