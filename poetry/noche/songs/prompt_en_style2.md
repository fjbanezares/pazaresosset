# Style of Music
Intimate midnight jazz, neo-soul, warm Rhodes piano, brushed snare drums, deep upright bass, smoky and tender female vocals ALWAYS IN FRONT AND DOMINANT, crystal clear English pronunciation, late night atmosphere, vulnerable and emotional, no aggressive beats, slow tempo
