# Style of Music
Organic French house, very subtle driving beat, uplifting soul-elevating melodies, hands in the air moments, warm atmospheric synthesizers, beautiful clear female vocals ALWAYS IN FRONT AND DOMINANT, pristine French pronunciation, massive emotional buildup, exquisite acoustic accordion and piano outro
