# Style of Music
Cinematic mid-tempo synthwave ballad, pulsing analog bass, atmospheric pads, deep emotional female vocals ALWAYS IN FRONT AND DOMINANT, crystal clear Spanish pronunciation, dramatic build-up, crying cello, heavy emotional drops
